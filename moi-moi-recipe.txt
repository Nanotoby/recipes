Ingredients
- Beans (the quantity depends on the individual)
- Maggi 
- Salt
- Pepper 
- Tomato
- Crayfish
- Onions
- Green Leafs


 Directions
- Blend the Beans in a blender.
- Blend the Pepper,Onions,Tomato all together.
- Mix both the blended quantities.
- Add salt, maggi and crayfish in your mixture.
- Fold your leafs in a way it can hold the mixture.
- Put in a pot to cook.
